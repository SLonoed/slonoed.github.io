Wishlist

Last Edited: October 26, 2024

If you want to give me a present but don't know what, give me a book, and I will be happy. I'm interested in a few kinds of books.

Book you love. It should be something you enjoyed a lot and want to share that joy.

Book you want to read. There are more books than I could read and picking the right one is a tough choice. If you already picked one, I want to read it.

Good book from your professional domain. Maybe you know chemistry and have found that rare, formula-filled, thousand-page manuscript that contains the best chemistry knowledge. I want it. I may not be able to understand it now but I might in the future.

Regarding book's condition. Most books I buy are used books. Knowing someone else touched it and shared the joy of reading warms my heart. If you have a dusty book, full of pencil marks and coffee spills, that you've read so many times that some pages are torn, that is the perfect gift for me.
