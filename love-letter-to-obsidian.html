Love Letter to Obsidian.md

Disclaimer: This blog post was created with the assistance of LLM

Last Edited: January 9, 2024

As a software engineer with a penchant for efficiency and security, I've navigated through various note-taking applications over the years. But it wasn't until I discovered Obsidian.md that I truly felt at home. Here's a glimpse into my journey and why Obsidian has become an indispensable part of my daily life.

My Quest for the Perfect Note-Taking App

My journey began with Apple Notes, a default choice given its integration with Apple devices. However, its reliance on Apple's ecosystem and lackluster keyboard support left me wanting more. I then turned to Notion, drawn by its robust features. Yet, the sluggish performance and a frustrating mobile experience quickly soured its appeal. FSNotes appeared as a beacon of simplicity, but its rigid structure and editing limitations soon became apparent.

Discovering Obsidian: A Turning Point

Enter Obsidian.md. It wasn't just another stop in my quest; it was the destination. What set Obsidian apart was not just its performance or the keyboard-centric design, which as a software engineer, I deeply appreciated. It was its philosophy of putting the user's data privacy and freedom at the forefront.

The local storage of data meant I had full control over my notes, free from the worry of external breaches. This, combined with the Markdown format, ensured that my data remained accessible and mine alone.

But it wasn't all about security. Obsidian's interface, designed for keyboard enthusiasts like myself, made navigating through notes a breeze. The seamless transition between editing and viewing, with Markdown syntax visible only around the cursor, was a game-changer.

What truly elevated Obsidian, though, was its plugin ecosystem. From daily notes to calendar integrations, each plugin felt like it was tailor-made for my workflow. The ease of crosslinking and the flat structure of notes encouraged a more natural, interconnected way of organizing my thoughts.

The Lasting Impact of Obsidian

A year into using Obsidian, I find myself more organized, more productive, and most importantly, more secure in my data management. It's not just a tool; it's a companion in my daily professional journey.

So, here's my love letter to Obsidian.md: a testament to a tool that not only met my expectations but exceeded them in every way possible. To those still searching for that perfect note-taking app, give Obsidian a try. It might just end your search, as it did mine.
